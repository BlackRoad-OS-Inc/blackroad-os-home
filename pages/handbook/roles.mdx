## Core roles

- **Product Steward** — frames problems, curates backlog, and keeps outcomes visible.
- **Engineering Lead** — steers technical direction, platform health, and incident response.
- **Design Partner** — champions usability, research, and accessibility.

## Decision rights

- Product stewards own **why** and **what success looks like**.
- Engineering leads own **how we build** and **when it is safe to ship**.
- Designers own **experience quality** and **customer clarity**.

## How to engage

- Start with a one-page RFC using the Governance > Decision Flow steps.
- Tag the right roles early; avoid late surprises.
- If ownership is unclear, open a thread in the #home-handbook channel.

## Growth signals

- Roles are succeeding when:
  - Work is shipped in small, observable slices.
  - Documentation is refreshed alongside releases.
  - Decisions are visible in the log with owners and rationale.
