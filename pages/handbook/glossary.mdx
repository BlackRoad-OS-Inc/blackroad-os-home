## Terms

- **ADR** — Architecture Decision Record; a short note capturing a technical decision and context.
- **Escalation window** — timebox for raising blockers before a decision auto-advances.
- **Guardrail** — a constraint that prevents risky behavior (e.g., required reviewers, 2FA).
- **Runbook** — step-by-step checklist to execute a repeatable process.
- **Snapshot** — concise update on a project’s status, risks, and next steps.

## Style guide

- Prefer plain language and verbs.
- Define acronyms on first use.
- Link to a template or decision log entry when referencing past work.
