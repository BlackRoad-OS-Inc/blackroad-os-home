## Principles

- **Default to transparency** — publish decisions and assumptions where others can find them.
- **Bias for shipping** — prefer small, reversible steps over large bets.
- **Kind candor** — feedback is timely, respectful, and tied to observable outcomes.

## Rituals

- **Weekly pulse** — share wins, blockers, and help requests every Monday.
- **Demo cadence** — short demos every other Thursday to celebrate progress.
- **Health checks** — quarterly survey on focus, clarity, and sustainability.

## Behaviors we reward

- Documenting before debating.
- Pulling peers into decisions early.
- Caring for reliability and safety as much as velocity.

## Collaboration norms

- Prefer async first; meetings are for decisions.
