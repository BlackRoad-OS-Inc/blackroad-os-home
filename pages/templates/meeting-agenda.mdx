---
title: Meeting Agenda Template
description: Reusable agenda skeleton with timeboxes and outcomes.
---

> Copy/paste into your calendar invite or doc.

- **Purpose**: _What decision or outcome do we need?_
- **Owner**: _Who is facilitating?_
- **Attendees**: _List roles or names._
- **Pre-read**: _Link to doc or pull request._

### Agenda (60 minutes)

1. Arrival & recap (5m)
2. Context + goal (10m)
3. Options & tradeoffs (20m)
4. Decision & owner (15m)
5. Next steps & comms (10m)

### Notes

- Decisions recorded as bullet points with owners.
- Capture risks, blockers, and follow-ups.
- Link to Governance > Decision Flow if further review is needed.
