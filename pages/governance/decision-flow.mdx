title: Decision Flow
description: Lightweight steps for proposing, validating, and recording decisions.
---

## Flow

1. **Frame** — Write a one-page RFC with problem, options, and guardrails.
2. **Signal** — Share in #decisions with an escalation window and requested roles.
3. **Debate** — Collect async comments; timebox to five working days.
4. **Decide** — Record the decision, owner, and effective date in the log.
5. **Broadcast** — Post a snapshot in the weekly pulse and link to the ADR.

## Decision log

Maintain a simple table with:

- Decision ID and title
- Owner and approvers
- Context, options, and chosen path
- Date and links to related runbooks

## Escalation

- If a decision stalls, the Product Steward names a decider and due date.
- Critical security changes escalate to the Security Champion within 24 hours.
