# 🏠 REPO: blackroad-os-home

**ROLE:** Company Home 🏠📚 – handbook, meeting templates, governance, and "how we work" operating system.

---

## 🎯 MISSION

- Be the **home base** for BlackRoad OS, Inc. – the place every human/agent lands first.
- Capture how we work, decide, communicate, and run the company.
- Turn chaos into clear rituals, templates, and governance.

---

## 🏗️ YOU OWN (✅)

### 📚 Handbook

- Vision, mission, values, and operating principles 🌟
- Org structure: teams, roles, agent types, responsibilities 🧭
- Ways of working: async rules, meeting norms, "how to escalate" 📡

### 🗓️ Rituals & Rhythms

- Daily/weekly/monthly/quarterly cadences 🌀
- Standup templates, retro templates, planning templates 📋
- OKR / goals process (how they're set, tracked, reviewed) 🎯

### 📓 Templates

- Meeting notes templates 📝
- Decision records / ADR templates 🧬
- Onboarding checklists for humans + agents 🚀

### 🏛️ Governance

- Who can change what (repos, domains, infra, money, policy) ⚖️
- Approval flows (e.g., high-risk changes, spend, legal) 🧾
- Links to core policies (security, privacy, communication) 🔐

## 🧪 LIVING DOCUMENT RULE

This repo is **alive**, not frozen:

- ✅ All processes must have an owner 🧑‍💼
- ✅ Changes should be tracked via PRs with clear summaries
- ✅ Big process changes = short "why we changed this" note 🧠

---

## 🔐 TRUST & COMPLIANCE

- Avoid storing personal sensitive info here (no real HR files) 🚫
- Store **policies**, not private records: the *rules*, not the *people*.
- Link out to secure systems when needed, don't duplicate them 🔗

---

## 📏 DESIGN PRINCIPLES

`blackroad-os-home` = **"how we work" OS**, not a random wiki:

- 🧭 Organize by flows: onboarding, running projects, shipping, escalations.
- 🎯 Every page should say who it's for and what problem it solves.

### Any new doc should answer:

1. 1️⃣ **Who is this for?** (role/agent type)
2. 2️⃣ **When do they use it?** (trigger/event)
3. 3️⃣ **What's the outcome if they follow it?**
