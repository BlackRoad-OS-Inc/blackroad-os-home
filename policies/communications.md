# Communications Policy

## Official Statements

- Official statements must be approved by the orchestrator and, when relevant, the Brand & Comms pod.
- Maintain a record of public announcements, press releases, and major posts for traceability.

## Speaking on Behalf of BlackRoad

- Only designated agents or the orchestrator may speak publicly for BlackRoad.
- Coordinate with Brand & Comms before publishing external content, including blog posts and marketing materials.

## Social Media Guidelines

- Keep public commentary respectful, factual, and consistent with company positions.
- Do not disclose confidential or regulated information on social platforms.
- Escalate controversial topics to the orchestrator before engaging.

## Documentation & Internal Comms

- Use shared repositories and journals for decisions and updates instead of private channels.
- Mark sensitive information clearly and restrict access according to data classification.
- Prefer asynchronous updates to reduce coordination overhead while keeping stakeholders informed.
