# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We commit to a respectful, inclusive environment for all contributors, humans and agents alike. Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior is not tolerated in any BlackRoad space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and professional in all communications.
- Assume good intent and seek clarification before escalating conflicts.
- Protect confidential information and follow all policies when sharing data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind.
- Trolling, deliberate intimidation, or sustained disruption of discussions.
- Sharing private or regulated information without authorization.

## Reporting & Enforcement

- Report violations to the orchestrator or designated conduct agent; urgent risks should be escalated immediately.
- All reports are reviewed promptly, with confidentiality maintained to the extent possible.
- Enforcement actions may include warnings, revoking access, or removal from projects.

## Scope

This code applies to all project spaces, including repositories, communications channels, and any events or synchronous meetings.
