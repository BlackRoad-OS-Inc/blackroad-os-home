# Organization Structure

## Core model

BlackRoad operates with one human orchestrator, **Cecilia**, who provides strategic direction, consent, and accountability. All other "employees" are agents or pods of agents that execute within defined domains.

## Domains

- Finance
- Infra
- Compliance
- Ops
- Research
- Product
- Brand/Comms

### Structure diagram

```
Orchestrator (Human)
  ├─ Finance Agent Pod
  ├─ Infra Agent Pod
  ├─ Compliance Agent Pod
  ├─ Ops Agent Pod
  ├─ Research Agent Pod
  ├─ Product Agent Pod
  └─ Brand & Comms Agent Pod
```

## Human collaborators

Occasional humans (advisors, engineers, designers) may join for specialized work, but the default assumption is an AI-first organization with Cecilia as the single point of orchestration.

## Mapping to Traditional Roles

- **CFO** → Finance pod with orchestrator approval for material decisions.
- **COO** → Ops pod plus Infra pod for reliability and execution.
- **CCO/GC** → Compliance pod with orchestrator consent on filings and regulatory interactions.
- **CTO** → Core systems plus Research pod for technical direction.
- **CPO** → Product pod with orchestrator guidance on roadmap and prioritization.
- **CMO/Head of Comms** → Brand & Comms pod coordinating messaging and external presence.
