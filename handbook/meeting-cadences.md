# Meeting Cadences

## Weekly Operator Review

- Agents prepare asynchronous packets covering metrics, incidents, and agent health.
- Orchestrator reviews within the scheduled window, responds with decisions or follow-ups, and journals significant outcomes.
- Review includes experiment status and planned changes for the coming week.

## Monthly Strategic Review

- Agents deliver FP&A forecasts, capital allocation proposals, and risk summaries.
- Orchestrator evaluates roadmap adjustments and approves major reallocations.
- Decisions and rationale are journaled with links to supporting analysis.

## Quarterly Big Picture

- Assess architecture, security posture, compliance readiness, and brand position.
- Stress-test assumptions about market, regulation, and technical direction.
- Identify structural improvements for agent pods and orchestrator tooling.

> Note: These cadences can be fully asynchronous; meetings are optional. The key is timely packets, clear review windows, and journaled outcomes.
